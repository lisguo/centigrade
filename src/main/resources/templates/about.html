About Centigrade

  • Our Mission

    Centigrade® is the most trusted recommendation source for quality entertainment. As the leading online aggregator of movie and TV show reviews from professional critics, Centigrade offers the most comprehensive guide to what's Hot. The world famous Fire score represents the percentage of positive professional reviews for films and TV shows and is used by millions of fans to help with their viewing decisions. Centigrade designates the best-reviewed movies and TV shows as Hot™. That accolade is awarded with a Fire score of 80% and higher and a required minimum number of reviews.
